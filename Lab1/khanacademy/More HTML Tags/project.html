Doner

  • Time: Approximately 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 1
Sauce 1
Lettuce as needed
Tomato 1
Lavash dough 2-4
Sour cucumber optional

Step 1: Grill or cook chicken breast.
Step 2: Put chicken, tomato and cucmber, and pour sauce
Step 3: Roll lavash and put it on the grill

Vegetable Fried Rice

  • Time: Approximately 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Cooked rice 2 cups
Mixed veggies (peas, carrots, corn) 1 cup
Eggs 2
Soy sauce 2 tbsp
Sesame oil 1 tbsp
Green onions as needed

Step 1: Scramble eggs in a wok; add mixed veggies.
Step 2: Stir in cooked rice, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
Step 3: Garnish with chopped green onions.

Tashkent Plov

  • Time: Approximately 120 minutes
  • Serves: 15-20
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 1000 grams
Meat 500 grams
Carrot 7
Oil 2 tbsp
Eggs 4-7
Salt and pepper to taste

Step 1: Fri meat and vegetables
Step 2: Add salt and pepper, add rice.
Step 3: Bake about 20-30 minutes.
